## Details

### #1 — Round starts without ready gating · OWNER 2.5/8.3 · S2 · OPEN
`ROUND_START` (and `PREPARATION_OPEN`) do not require players to be `isReady`. The lobby ready/mic-check (Story 2.5) is informational only; the facilitator can start regardless. Decide whether starting should be gated on all participants ready (or keep it facilitator-discretion and make the readiness purely advisory). Not introduced by 8.11.

### #2 — Lobby Defuser choice ignored · OWNER 8.3/2.4 · S2 · OPEN
The relay rotation (`relayOrder[currentDefuserIndex]`) is the sole authority for who defuses each round (Story 8.3 decision). A facilitator who assigns the "defuser" role to a specific player in the lobby sees that choice overridden by rotation in round 1. Either remove the lobby Defuser-role affordance (assign team only; rotation picks the Defuser) or make round-1's Defuser honour the lobby pick. UX decision for 8.3/2.4. Related to #3.

### #3 — relayOrder ordering · OWNER 2.4 · S2 · OPEN
Players appear to rotate in alphabetical (display-name) order rather than join/assignment order. Verify how `relayOrder` is built in `assignPlayerToTeam` (Story 2.4) — it should be append-on-assign (chronological). If it sorts by name or playerId, that's the bug. (With the alphabetically-named bot swarm the two orders coincide, so reproduce with human players or non-alphabetical names.)

### #4 — Resting-team copy differs by phase · OWNER 8.11 · S3 · OPEN
During an active round a resting player sees `RESTING_SPECTATE` ("Resting this round — watch the other team defuse."); between rounds they see the Scoreboard's between-rounds waiting copy ("Round over. Stand by for the next one."). Both are phase-correct but read as two different states. Optional polish: unify the resting/waiting voice so the resting experience feels continuous. Low priority; behaviour is correct.

### #5 — Interaction allowed while paused · OWNER 8.7 · S1 · **FIXED 2026-06-21**
`MODULE_INTERACT` never checked `session.pausedAt`, so during a pause the Defuser could keep cutting wires — including landing the 3rd strike to detonate and resolve the round, defeating the whole point of the freeze. **Fix:** `apps/server/src/handlers/moduleHandlers.ts` now refuses `MODULE_INTERACT` with `SESSION_PAUSED` when `session.pausedAt !== null` (checked right after the session load, before the bomb read). Covered by a new test in `moduleHandlers.test.ts` (a 2-strike bomb stays at 2 strikes, no `MODULE_UPDATE`/`BOMB_EXPLODED`). Note: the bot swarm can't currently drive the resume (bots don't ready-up after a *disconnect* pause), so Jay couldn't exercise the resume path — tracked separately as a sim-harness enhancement, not a code bug.

### #6 — Detonation on the scoreboard + scoring concern · OWNER 8.5/8.6/8.10 · S1 · OPEN
`resolveRound` records `elapsedMs` into `cumulativeTimeMs` for every outcome, but the between-rounds breakdown renders only the time, never a pass/fail marker (the outcome IS persisted in `RoundState.outcomes` / `ScoreboardPayload.failedTeams`). Two problems:
1. **No visual win/loss per round** — a detonation looks identical to a slow defuse.
2. **Scoring inconsistency (the real risk):** a **timeout** records the **full** `timerMs`, but a **3-strike detonation** records the **elapsed-at-detonation** (often *less*). On a "lowest cumulative time wins" contest, a *fast* detonation can beat a slow defuse. A failed round likely needs a defined penalty (full `timerMs`, or explicit DQ), not elapsed-at-detonation.

Decide the failed-round scoring rule and the per-round pass/fail rendering in Story 8.10 (final scoreboard + scoring); reflect it in the 8.6 preview.

### #7 — No return-to-lobby / end after a finished run · OWNER 8.10 · S2 · OPEN (not yet implemented)
At relay completion the Scoreboard shows the "relay complete" notice and refuses further advance (`RELAY_COMPLETE`) — but there is no affordance to end the session, show a final scoreboard, or return to the lobby for another run. That transition is **Story 8.10** (scoring, final scoreboard, session-end persistence), not yet implemented. Expected gap.

### #8 — Resume button has no text · OWNER 8.7 · S2 · **FIXED 2026-06-21**
The `PauseOverlay` Resume (and disconnect "I'm ready") buttons used the Tailwind class `text-surface-base`, but **no `--color-surface-base` token exists** (the tokens are `surface`, `surface-raised`, `surface-manual`). The undefined utility left the text colour inheriting the strip's cream `ink-primary` → cream text on a cream (`bg-ink-primary`) button = invisible. **Fix:** `apps/client/src/ui/PauseOverlay.tsx` now uses the real `text-surface` token (dark on cream). The Pause CTA was fine (it uses `text-ink-muted`), which is why only Resume looked blank.
